Rock Paper Scissors Game

Play the game via the console in the web browser.

  1. Right Click and select "Inspect" or use "Ctrl+Shift+I" (Cmd+Shift+I on Mac)
  2. Select "Console" in the top of the inspector. It's next to Elements.
  3. Click the 'Play Game' button.
  4. The results of each round will be displayed in the console.
  5. Press the 'Play Game' button again to play again.
